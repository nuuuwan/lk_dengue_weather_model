🇱🇰 DENGUE WEATHER RISK MODEL — SRI LANKA 🇱🇰

☔️ Rain + 🥵 Heat = 🦟 Dengue

I implemented the model from "Dengue outbreak prediction model for urban Colombo using meteorological data" (Erandi et al., 2021) using real-time @OpenMeteo weather data, and extended it to all 333 MOH regions across Sri Lanka — mapping current risk plus 2- and 4-week forecasts. 

DETAILS: <https://github.com/nuuuwan/lk_dengue_weather_model>

RESEARCH PAPER: <https://github.com/nuuuwan/lk_dengue_weather_model/blob/main/research_papers/%5B2021%20Erandi%20et%20al%5D%20Dengue%20outbreak%20prediction%20model%20for%20urban%20Colombo%20using%20meteorological%20data.pdf>
